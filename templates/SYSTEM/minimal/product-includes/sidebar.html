Our exceptional high quality leather products are produced with Full Grain leather due to its thick pull-up leather attributes. Full grain leather is known to be the topmost layer of the cowhide, the most durable and highly valued skin of the animal. In comparison to genuine leather, full grain leather is very durable and long-lasting. Over time and usage, full grain leather ages to become soft and supple, resulting in a product that is able to withstand daily wear and tear, while providing an exceptional luxury experience.
